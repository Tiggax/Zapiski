kjer generator naključnih števil vrne naslednje zapredje:

| 8   | 2   | 3   | 7   | 13  | 12  | 26   | 19  | 
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | ---- | --- |
| 110 | 10  | 10  | 0   | 0   | 0   | 1110 |     |
